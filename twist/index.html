What is a scene

All the world's a stage - let's rehearse together

Every time we interview for a position, pitch an idea, present at a meeting or sit on a panel, it is a chance to make an impact on peers, bosses, and potential clients and customers. Each of these interactions is something we can rehearse together. On scene.ly, we call these interactions "scenes." scene.ly creates a virtual online space for you to rehearse these scenes with a peer from your industry

How to become a coach

Anyone is welcome to apply to be a scene.ly coach. We vet all of our coaches for proper fit and offer coach training. Below are the guidelines we use.

Work Experience

Our coaches range in experience from work as an intern, a couple years in industry, seasoned professional, SME, Senior Executive, or retiree.

Coaches Orientation

All coaches go through an orientation and are required to sign off on scene.ly's "Coaches Rules and Responsibilities" reinforcing our committment to creating safe spaces, vulnerable moments, and constructive feedback

Certifications (optional)

We do take coaches with industry certifications and professional coaching experience.

scene.ly Certification

We offer a coaching certification course, a virtual two-day course given by an accredited executive coaching firm for those looking to take their coaching skills to the next level

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you get started

What is a scene?

A scene is an online video interaction between an individual and a scene coach of their choosing

How do I "make a scene?"

When someone finds a coach they would like to work with, they can reserve 15 minute increments of time through the coach's listing page. Once you reserve time, the coach will be notified and reach out to the buyer to coordinate a mutually convenient time

Who can become a coach?

We believe anyone that has been active in the workforce has experience that can be shared and used for the benefit of others. However scene.ly believes coaches must create a safe space where people can truly ask any question they like, are willing to be vulnerable to encourage others to do the same, and are willing to share their experience facing a similar challenge as their scene partner. Anyone can apply to be a coach, but all coaches are vetted and required to sign a coach's rules and responsibilities agreement

What is the process if I want to book a coach?

Browse the listings of coaches on the site, pick one you like, and click the reserve button to send a payment. Then you can communicate through scene.ly direct messages to coordinate a mutually convenient time. Once the session over each of you will have a chance to provide feedback, rate each other, and mark the transaction as complete

Are coaches certified?

There is no requirement for a coach to be certified. scene.ly believes many of us have the power to coach others simply by participating in the workforce and learning norms and conventions at different organizations. Certified coaches are welcome, and scene.ly does offer a certification course for those who would like to add credentials to their offering

What if I have an issue with my scene?

While we try to make the process seamless, there may be a time when you encouter an issue. Either someone will not make an appointment or there is a technical issue. Please reach us any time through our contact form or send an email to us at info@scene.ly any time
